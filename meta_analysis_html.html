Hispanic/Latinx persons vs.White persons

Data Frame

Use effect measures provided in effect_measures.xlsx file.

data<-data.frame(
  study=c("Raphael et al. 2021","Zhu et al. 2019","Mark et al. 2021"), #specify study names
  TE=log(c(1.52,0.827631579,1.236601467)), #input risk ratios (provided in study or calculated in Excel)
  seTE=c(0.218346583,0.45879072,0.120959542) # input standard errors (calculated in Excel)
)

Pooled Analysis

meta<-metagen(
  TE=data$TE,
  seTE=data$seTE,
  studlab=data$study,
  sm="RR",
  common = FALSE,
  random = TRUE,
  title="Latinx/Hispanic vs. White"
)
